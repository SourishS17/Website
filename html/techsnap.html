TechSnap! Your snap of tech whenever I feel like writing one! Here, I'll take the latest news; Twitter, YouTube, News sites - wherever I happen to see it first. I'll summarise what the news itself is, and give some comments, opinions, and analysis in a digestable mind-dump format. Hopefully, there'll be a couple a day (on average). Yes, I know, I truly am the pioneer of online news distribution . Send any comments, ideas, feedback, or questions to techsnap@sourishsharma.com.
